Section 1: Expert help at each step

There are several ways you can bring a new person into your team depending on the roleâ€™s requirements. Help is available at each stage, ensuring that you recruit the best person for your vacancy.

 

Your HR department

When you need to fill a position or create a role, contact your HR department in the first instance. They will advise on next steps. If there are significant changes in the role, for example, HR can help you to shape the job description and ensure the position is at the correct grade.

 

Departmental Resourcing Group (DRG)

The Departmental Resourcing Group (DRG) runs recruitment campaigns for all roles across the Department (Dpt) and its executive agencies up to and including Grade 6. It provides an expert service to help you to find the best candidate for your role. A dedicated DRG advisor will work with you to determine the best approach for your campaign.

 

Government Recruitment Service (GRS)

Government Recruitment Service (GRS) provides a central service across government and will be in contact with you at various stages of your campaign. GRS is responsible for:

  • launching your job adverts on the applicant tracking system (ATS), so your vacancy is visible to candidates on Civil Service Jobs
  • communicating with the vacancy holder
  • communicating with candidates, including issuing sift and interview results, and inviting candidates to interview
  • undertaking pre-employment checks on behalf of Dpt
