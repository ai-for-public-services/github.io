Section 17: Data protection

You will collect a lot of sensitive information relative to candidates when recruiting. It is essential that you handle any data securely and only for the purposes of the recruitment process.

Data should be securely destroyed once its purpose has expired. You should do this consistently across all your devices.

Read the data protection (GDPR) guidance on your intranet before handling sensitive candidate data.

Recruitment documents (such as interview documents) with candidate details should be held for 3 months and then destroyed. Oleeo vX will hold other recruitment information for 2 years.
